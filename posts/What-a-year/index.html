What a year! 2020 Here I come

Posted Dec 25, 2019 2019-12-25T05:50:00+08:00 by KaleF04

Hey there! Seems like you’re interested in what I want to share :D

Let me start off by saying thank you for being interested in what I want to share! Not everyone seems to be interested enough. D: So, okay. Enough with the chitchat!

The year well started off good, I would say. Everything was doing alright, until the Q2 of 2019. Everything went downhill for me; But look at me now :D You wouldn’t be reading this if that never had happened, Hah! During the Q2 of 2019, I did some pretty stupid things that’s for sure. No time for regrets now tho. XD Yes, I’m very sorry for using this emoji, it’s not me yes, but let me use it for once in public, hehe. I did some pretty interesting stuff during mid 2019 too, I learned Ethical hacking, that’s off the bucket list now. I also learned how to play the Ukelele! I can’t play any songs nor strum upwards properly, but that’s a plus(?) HAHAHA. Anyways I did explore a lot of things this 2019, this includes music production! Okay, let me say this now. I didn’t do very good, but I’m happy with what I made, others? Not so much but, alright HAHAHA. Okay, update about the game? It seems to be going nowhere at this point HAHAHA but I would continue it next year, hopefully finish it without procrastination anymore, I don’t like procrastinating but what can I do anyways? A question I would answer later.

I also hoped into extra curricular activities, such as running as treasurer in the student council, I didn’t win but what an experience though! XD I also hoped into a sport I never thought I would, and that was airsoft, and it was so much fun, I want to go on another one! Maybe next time though. Something unexpected happened too. I was in the honor list?? Yes, I was. I feel sad I got in. I don’t think I deserve the spot; What can I do anyways? It was given to me, anyways, I’m thankful for being there, I’ll keep trying my best to maintain or even do better! Okay, that’s not me, but let me express myself HAHAHA

One thing I’ve noticed in me so far is that I became more open to people! Which is sad since I’m not used to it, I sometimes over express myself, I think. It’s sad I don’t want to bother anyone whilst (I hope I used that right HAHAHA) wanting to share what I’ve dont, I mean I don’t have anyone even HAHAHA with that said. I don’t know why but I try to supress it, but that doesn’t happen unless I’m very comfortable talking to whoever it is. HAHAHA okay, enough with my character development! But as of right now, I’m not suppressing it, if I was you wouldn’t be able to read this HAHAHA

Looking back this decade, yes, were at this conversation! I’ve done so much! But, it’s sad at the same time, I don’t want to grow old this quick. All the small things this decade are enough to make me tear up. Time flies so quickly, it’s sad. I mean, I can’t do anything about it. I try to cope with it as best as I can though, but at times, it just goes out. whispers: Retreat 2019

With that mentioned. It’s gonna be a hard 2020 to start off, I’m not ready to have a new beginning yet. I’ve spent 13 years at the same school. For reference, as of 2019, I’m 16 years old. I’m not ready to leave the people who have been there since the beginning nor the end. If one of you does read this. I’ll really miss you guys. I can’t gurantee that we’ll be in touch in the next few years; But here’s one thing, I’ll always look back to our fun memories. teary eyes. Honestly, I still hope we would be in touch.

Oh, here’s one too. As of my writing. Dec 25, 2019. Merry Christmas peeps!

I think were halfway through the article HAHAHA. Let’s move on with Q3-Q4 of 2019! Let’s just say this, I’ve done some stupid things through out this quarter HAHAHA. More things to make me tear up! D:

Reading this article, the arrangement seems so random. Oh, well. Let’s talk about 2020! I have plenty of plans this 2020. This includes finishing the 3? or 2 year old game! It’s frustrating I’m not good with pixel art. Not just the game too, I want to explore music production once again, and hopefully make one track? HAHAHA then, probably publish the software if it’s ready to go! With plans in mind. Let’s talk school, it’s definitely gonna be a lonely year, but I hope to make friends. I just hope my shyness don’t get the best of me HAHAHA. uh, I’m running out of things to talk about. Help? HAHAHA

Okay, plan for 2020? Make this site better; Make it official too. Sana ol official HAHAHA

Let’s end it off there. I might delete this after sometime, but okay. HAHAHA
